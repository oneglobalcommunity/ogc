Owner's Story

Chef Kim's Journey

Chef Kim immigrated to the United States in 2005 with a dream of sharing authentic Korean flavors with the community. Growing up in Seoul, he learned traditional cooking techniques from his grandmother, who ran a small restaurant in their neighborhood.

After working in various restaurants and saving every penny, Chef Kim opened Daebak in 2015. The name "Daebak" means "awesome" or "the best" in Korean, reflecting his commitment to excellence and his passion for Korean cuisine.

"I want people to experience the same warmth and love I felt growing up in my grandmother's kitchen. Every dish tells a story of our culture and traditions."
